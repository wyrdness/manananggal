## Respect & Ethical Considerations

> **Note on Sources**: Many traditional beliefs are held within oral traditions and living communities. Researchers should engage with Filipino communities respectfully, recognizing these are not merely "superstitions" but part of active cultural heritage. Consultation with Filipino cultural experts and community elders is recommended for deeper understanding.
